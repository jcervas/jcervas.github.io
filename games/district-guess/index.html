How to Play

Identify the 119th Congress congressional district shown on the map.

  • You have 5 guesses per day.
  • A new district appears every day — same for all players.
  • Pick a state, then a district number from the dropdowns.
  • After each wrong guess, a new clue is revealed.
  • Your timer starts when you submit your first guess.
  • Come back tomorrow for a new district!

Clues revealed in order

  1. Geographic region + terrain map (elevation only, no labels)
  2. Primary time zone
  3. Largest racial / ethnic group
  4. Median household income
  5. Full labeled map — cities revealed before your final guess

Bonus: Once you guess the correct state, it locks and you only need to pick the district number.
