How to Play

Identify the congressional district shown on the map using the 2026 district boundaries.

  • You have 6 guesses per day.
  • A new district appears every day — same for all players.
  • Click a state on the reference map to guess it first.
  • Once you nail the state, pick the district number.
  • Your timer starts when you submit your first guess.
  • Come back tomorrow for a new district!

Hot / Cold elimination

Each wrong state guess tells you something geographic:

  • Hot / Adjacent — the correct state borders the one you guessed. Only neighboring states stay in play.
  • Cold / Not adjacent — the correct state is far away. The guessed state and all its neighbors are eliminated.

Eliminated states grey out on the map and chip list automatically.
